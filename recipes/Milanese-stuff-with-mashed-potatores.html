Milanese stuffed with mashed potatoes

Do you love the homemade Milanese recipe with mashed potatoes? Then these Milanesas Stuffed with Mashed Potatoes will become your favorite dish. And what better than to combine the garnish with the main course? Enjoy these Milanesas Stuffed with Mashed Potatoes for the next meal you are going to prepare.

    Ingredients

  • 2 liters water, for the puree
  • 4 potatoes, cut into cubes, the puree
  • 1 cup cow's milk, for the puree
  • 2 tablespoons butter, for the puree
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white pepper, for the puree
  • 1 tablespoon nutmeg, for the puree
  • 1/2 cups cow's milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1/2 kilos beef cutlet
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • parsley, chopped, to serve

      • Steps

    1. For the mashed potatoes, heat a pot over medium heat with the water, cook the potatoes with a little salt until they are soft, strain and reserve.
    2. Heat a pot over medium heat with the milk, add butter and the cooked potatoes, season with salt, pepper and nutmeg. With the help of a masher crush the potatoes to form the puree, cook until the milk is reduced and obtain a smooth puree, chill.
    3. On a board spread the beef fillets and stuffed with mashed potatoes, form well compressed rolls and reserve.
    4. Beat the milk with the egg and add salt and pepper. Pass and press the Milanese rolls through the egg mixture and the bread crumbs.
    5. In a pan, heat the oil and fry the rolls until they are crisp, remove the oil and place them on absorbent paper, reserve.
    6. Cut into slices and serve with chopped parsley. Enjoy!.
